Minigame - RoboSort

Instructions: Collect as many robot pieces as possible using the correct collector box & arm. Collecting batteries recharges your battery (lives). Your battery also loses charge every so often with a maximum lifetime of 1 minute 30 seconds. You can control the gamespeed with the arrow keys.

Keybinds: Motor Collector - 1 | Battery Collector - 2 | Gears Collector - 3 | Wheels Collector - 4 | Speed Up - Arrow Up | Speed Down - Arrow Down
